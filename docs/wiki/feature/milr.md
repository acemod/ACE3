## 1. Overview
IR laser attachment with built-in rangefinder, ballistic computer and zeroable laser.


## 2. Usage
- Point weapon at target and tap <kbd>Tab</kbd> for rangefinding. (Long-hold <kbd>Tab</kbd> to clear).
- The device displays range and computed drop (in MRAD). Targets too far or too close will show `---`.
- The IR laser auto adjusts and will be zeroed for the computed range. A zeroing out of range will show `!!!`.
